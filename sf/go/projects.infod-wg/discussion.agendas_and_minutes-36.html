As agreed on the call on 6 August, we'll have weekly INFOD calls during August . On 13 August we'll cover INFOD

Minutes of 13th of August TeleConference
Attendees: Susan Malaika, Chris Kantarjiev, Steve Fisher,William Vambenepe, Abdeslem Djaoui, Dieter Gawlick, David Berry
, and Vijay Dialani

Points of discussion:

  a.. Relation to OGSA and WS Notification (WSN) - Susan provided a thorough
update on the current state of OGSA and WSN notification models. The group
briefly discussed the notification models with the OGSA and WSN
representatives. Detailed discussions have been scheduled to take place on
27th of August 2004. Participants for the 27th of August tele-conference may
wish to familiarize themselves with the background material. Please, refer
to the
useful links (curtsey Susan) at the end of this mail.
  b.. Use cases - Steve and Susan and still working on the document and
intend to have a first draft for the next regular conference call
  c.. Specification - Chris intends to have a first draft for the next
regular conference call.
  d.. Usage Patterns - Vijay intends to circulate the draft for discussions
at next regular conference call.
  e.. GGF-12 - A F2F discussion has been scheduled for the Thursday morning
at GGF-12. Thanks to Susan for arranging the session.
Action items:
  a.. Chris, Vijay and Susan to submit the documents as submission towards
GGF-12.

Minutes from 23 April call
Participants:

    * Vijay Dialani
    * Steve Fisher
    * Susan Malaika
    * Shailendra Mishra
    * Norman Paton


Apologies:

    * Dave Berry
    * Dieter Gawlick
    * Chris Kantarjiev
    * Inderpal Narang



Next Calls:

    * Friday 30 April at 8 or 9am pacific : INFOD folk will join the OGSA Data call on Friday 30 April. This is an 
extended call run by Dave Berry to work on OGSA Data Scenarios - We'll post the details of the OGSA Data call to this 
mailing list when we have them. In the meantime see the OGSA Data minutes and activities so far can be seen here
          o https://forge.gridforum.org/docman2/ViewCategory.php?group_id=42&category_id=708
          o https://forge.gridforum.org/docman2/ViewCategory.php?group_id=42&category_id=710
    * Friday 7 May at 9am pacific : A note will be circulated on this mailing list with the INFOD call details and 
agenda


Actions

    * 1. Dieter and Susan to request from GGF  
          o - that GGF consider INFOD to be a formal GGF group (the INFOD BOF was run at GGF10)
          o - that INFOD have one session at GGF11 to cover these three topics:
                + *a* The INFOD specification document
                + *b* INFOD Scenarios
                + *c* Relationships with and differentiation from other groups and activities inside and outside GGF, e.
g., DAIS and WS-Notification  
          o - the GGF INFOD session should not clash with DAIS and with any OGSA Data meetings (ASIDE: are there any 
other sessions an INFOD session should not clash with?) 

    * 2. It was suggested that Chris Kantarjiev (INFOD GridForge WebMaster) move INFOD related documents from DAIS to 
INFOD on Gridforge. 

    * 3. Norman Paton and Dieter Gawlick to comment on a first attempt at listing the INFOD related documents that 
currently appear on the DAIS Website and that are candidates for Chris to move to INFOD:
          o Information Dissemination in the Grid Environment (also known as Data Distribution in the Grid Environment).
 This document will form the starting point for an INFOD specification
            https://forge.gridforum.org/docman2/ViewProperties.php?group_id=49&category_id=517&document_content_id=1794
          o Grid Data Distribution Charts https://forge.gridforum.org/docman2/ViewProperties.php?group_id=49&category_id
=607&document_content_id=1543 
          o DAIS and Information Dissemination Usage Scenarios (should this document be moved?) https://forge.gridforum.
org/docman2/ViewProperties.php?group_id=49&category_id=517&document_content_id=1806
          o Any others?

    * 3. Chris Kantarjiev to post these notes to INFOD Gridforge (after people have had a chance to comment)

    * 4. Vijay Dialani will take a look at the Information Dissemination in the Grid Environment document (mentioned 
above) in order to order suggest items that need work for GGF11

    * 5. All to participate in the OGSA Data Scenario activity on 30 April




Items of interest:

    * The proposed INFOD charter was discussed on the INFOD call, and the people on the call agreed it looked OK. See
          o https://forge.gridforum.org/docman2/ViewCategory.php?group_id=139&category_id=697
          o https://forge.gridforum.org/projects/infod-wg
    * Deadlines:
          o GGF Working Drafts submission deadline for GGF11 is 14 May 2004
          o GGF session requests deadline for GGF11 is 7 May 2004  
    * OGSA Data activities information is here:
          o https://forge.gridforum.org/docman2/ViewCategory.php?group_id=42&category_id=708
          o https://forge.gridforum.org/docman2/ViewCategory.php?group_id=42&category_id=710
